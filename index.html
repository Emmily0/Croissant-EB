🇫🇷 A French Pastry Delight 🇫🇷

A croissant is a buttery, flaky, and crescent-shaped pastry originating from France, made with layers of leavened dough.
Interestingly, its roots trace back to Austria, where the crescent-shaped **"kipferl"** inspired this French classic.

Today, the croissant is a symbol of French culture and baking excellence.
It is a popular choice for breakfast or as a snack, enjoyed plain or filled with sweet or savory ingredients.

Almond croissants, chocolate-filled **pain au chocolat**, or savory variations like ham and cheese croissants are just a few ways people around the world enjoy this versatile pastry.
